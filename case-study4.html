Tableau Dashboard: G20 Consumer Behavior Insights

Tableau is a powerful data visualization tool that allows organizations to analyze complex data and present it in an easy-to-understand format. For this project, we analyzed consumer behavior data from G20 countries, using Tableau to present insights that would help businesses make informed decisions based on global market trends.

The Challenge: Analyzing Global Consumer Behavior

With consumer behavior constantly evolving, especially in emerging markets, it became increasingly important for businesses to understand trends and preferences at a global scale. The challenge was to process vast amounts of data, from various sources, and to deliver meaningful insights that would help brands make decisions about their products and services.

The Solution: Tableau Data Visualization

We used Tableau to create interactive dashboards that provided easy access to complex consumer behavior data. These dashboards were designed to give businesses a clear understanding of regional preferences, spending patterns, and purchasing behaviors, with the ability to filter data by country, income level, and product category.
